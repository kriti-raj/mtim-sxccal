About Us

Modern Trends in Microbiology is the flagship event of the Department of Microbiology, St Xavier's College Autonomous, Kolkata. This acclaimed international seminar was conceptualised by our Convenor, Dr. Arup Kr. Mitra and executed by the entire department, every year since the last two decades. Over the years we have had the pleasure of attending informative sessions delivered by several exceptional speakers from different institutes, in and outside Kolkata. This has reinforced our belief in interdepartmental relationships. As we step into the 20th year of this educational extravaganza, we invite all the colleges across Kolkata to join us in this illuminative journey of learning through events like Paper Presentation, Poster Presentations and several others to be hosted in our College Campus on 6th and 7th of October, 2023 Paper Presentation "No research without action, No action without research" The true purpose of any scientific study, lies in the art of proving and disproving theories through practical application. So we bring to you, the meticulous art of Paper Presentation. It's an exclusive event for the students of St.Xavier's College, Autonomous Kolkata. Participants will be writing as well as presenting research papers adhering to the topic Discovery, Novelty and Aspirations in the field of microbiology. Both original as well as review papers are welcome. Poster Presentation “Research is seeing what everybody else has seen and thinking what nobody else has thought." The art of Poster Presentation helps to visually depict the scientific approach on almost any and every theme. Participants will present a poster designed originally by them adhering to the topic Discovery, Novelty and Aspirations in the field of microbiology. Quiz To be or not to be might be the question, but for this quiz, the questions will ring and revolve around your love for Biology. A classic Quiz with three rounds where 8 teams will fight it out till one remains in the end, proving again Darwin's laws of Natural Selection. Photography takes an instant out of time, altering life by holding it still. With this philosophy in mind we present to you our new event for this year. Participants will be submitting pictures on the topic that will be declared a week before the registration dates. The theme remains broadly Biology They will be judged on the uniqueness of the picture, adherence to theme and relevance to the topic.

Founder and Convenor of MTIM

Dr. Arup Kumar Mitra

I, the Convener of Modern Trends In Microbiology, is really proud of the fact that this event of the Department of Microbiology, St. Xavier’s College(Autonomous), Kolkata has become a testament to the fact that diligence reaps the sweet fruits of success. In the 20th year since its inception MTIM; the representative of the department, has proved its mettle. The immense enthusiasm of my fellow students and persistent efforts of my fellow colleagues and support staff makes the event a greater success each passing year. To commemorate the 20th year we have also planned for an alumni outreach through a dinner reception, to celebrate and revisit the cherished memories together. This two-day seminar is aimed to inculcate scientific thinking in students and help them strive for excellence. The journey from inter-departmental event to a national seminar has been an arduous yet fulfilling one, two decades worth of experience and memories aims us to reach for greater heights. I would also like to thank Father Principal, Vice Principal, respected Dean of Arts and Science for allowing us to organize this event. The zeal that I witness amidst the corridors of the department makes me sure, we have created a legacy and it will live on.

Wishing all the best and a successful MTIM XX, Dr. Arup Kumar Mitra
